## Step-by-Step Licensure Guide

Maine is an NLC state. Single-state endorsement takes **2-4 weeks**.

### Step 1: Complete MSN or DNP with PMHNP Focus

Graduate from an accredited advanced practice program. Must complete **45 contact hours** of advanced pharmacology.

### Step 2: Pass the ANCC PMHNP-BC Exam

Maintain active national certification.

| Membership Status | Exam Fee |
|:---|:---|
| Non-member | $395 |
| ANA member | $295 |
| AANP member | $340 |

Learn more about the [PMHNP-BC credential](/blog/what-does-pmhnp-bc-mean)

### Step 3: Apply for Your ME RN License

Maine is a **Nurse Licensure Compact (NLC) state**. If your primary state of residence is another NLC state, your multistate RN license covers you here.

### Step 4: Apply for Your ME APRN License

Submit your APRN application to the Maine State Board of Nursing.

### Step 5: Complete 24-Month Transition to Practice

New APRNs must practice for **24 months** under:
- Supervision of a licensed physician or supervising NP, **or**
- Employment at a clinic/hospital with a physician medical director

After submitting written evidence of completion, you gain full independence.

### Step 6: Register with the DEA

Prescriptive authority is **included in your licensure**. Register with the DEA for controlled substances.

- **Fees:** ~$888 for 3 years

### Step 7: Apply for an NPI Number

Obtain your National Provider Identifier (NPI) via the [CMS NPPES system](https://nppes.cms.hhs.gov/) (Free).

## Prescriptive Authority in ME

**What can PMHNPs prescribe?**
Prescriptive authority for legend drugs and controlled substances is **included inherently** in your licensure.

**Schedule II-V rules:**
Full prescribing of **Schedule II through V** — prescribe, administer, dispense, and procure.

**Prescribing gap rules:**

| Gap in Prescribing | Additional Pharmacology CE Required |
|:---|:---|
| **2+ years** | 15 contact hours |
| **5+ years** | 45 contact hours |

## Telehealth Rules in ME

**Can PMHNPs do telehealth?**
Yes — Maine offers broad Medicaid coverage for home-based care and relies heavily on telehealth for rural and coastal communities.

**Out-of-state requirements:**
Maine is an NLC member. The **APRN Compact is not enacted** — out-of-state PMHNPs must hold an active **Maine APRN license**.

**Controlled substance teleprescribing:**
Federal DEA and HHS flexibilities have been extended through **December 31, 2027**.

## Tips for PMHNPs Moving to ME

1. **Log Your Transition Hours:** Carefully document your first 24 months under supervision or within a facility with a medical director for seamless transition to full independence.

2. **Track Your Prescribing History:** If you stop prescribing for 2+ years, Maine requires extra pharmacology CE (15-45 hours) before you can resume.

3. **Complete Opioid CEs:** Prescribers must complete **3 CE hours** in opioid prescribing every renewal cycle, in addition to 50 total CE hours.

4. **Capitalize on the Salary Premium:** Maine's psych-specific salary is nearly **$90K higher** than the general NP average — use this in contract negotiations.
